YodhaPlay Fair Play Policy

At YodhaPlay, we are committed to maintaining a fair and competitive environment for all participants. This Fair Play Policy outlines the rules and guidelines designed to uphold integrity, promote honest gameplay, and ensure a level playing field. By participating in YodhaPlay, you agree to adhere to these policies. Violations may result in penalties, including prizes forfeiture, account suspension, or permanent bans.

  1. Reason for Removal from Custom Room: You were removed due to non-compliance with match rules & regulations.
  2. Reporting Hackers or Cheaters: Report suspicious gameplay to customer support for investigation.
  3. Teaming Up in Solo Matches: Collaborating with other players in solo matches is strictly prohibited and results in the forfeiture of prizes.
  4. Action on Reports Without Valid Proof: Reports submitted without sufficient evidence will not be considered.
  5. Reasons for a Permanent Account Ban, Rejection of Any Pending Withdrawal Requests, and Permanent Revocation of Withdrawal Access: Behaving inappropriately with customer support, using unauthorized tools, operating multiple accounts, or exceeding the daily match participation limits may lead to these penalties.

By following this Fair Play Policy, you contribute to a safe and enjoyable gaming experience for everyone. If you have any questions or need assistance, contact our support team.
