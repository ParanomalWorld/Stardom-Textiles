YodhaPlay Privacy Policy

Welcome to the YodhaPlay Privacy Policy, where we detail how we collect, use, and protect your personal information to ensure a seamless, secure, and trustworthy experience. By using our platform, you agree to the practices outlined in this policy, designed to safeguard your data while enhancing your interaction with our services.

1. Information Collection and Use

To provide an optimized experience, we may request personal information such as your name, email address, and mobile number. This data is securely stored and used in accordance with this policy to improve and personalize your experience. Additionally, we collaborate with trusted third-party services including Google Play Services, AdMob, and Firebase Analytics, which may collect further data to enhance service quality.

2. Log Data

To ensure seamless functionality and address any issues, we collect diagnostic data in the event of app errors. This log data may include your device's IP address, name, operating system version, app configuration at the time of use, timestamps, and other technical details. This information helps us resolve errors efficiently and maintain service quality.

3. Cookies

While our app does not explicitly use cookies, certain third-party services integrated into our platform may utilize them to improve their functionality. Cookies are small data files used as unique identifiers. You can choose to accept or decline these cookies, although refusal may limit access to some features.

4. Service Providers

We work with third-party service providers to support and enhance our platform. These providers assist in hosting, analytics, and other essential services. While they may have access to your personal information to perform their tasks, they are bound by confidentiality agreements and are prohibited from using your data for unauthorized purposes.

5. Security

Your privacy and security are paramount to us. We use industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute protection. Rest assured, we are constantly improving our security protocols to minimize risks.
