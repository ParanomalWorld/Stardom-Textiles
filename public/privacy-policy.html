Privacy Policy

Introduction

Welcome to YodhaPlay, a skill-based entertainment app for Android offering interactive challenges and secure wallet top-ups for enhanced user experiences. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information in compliance with the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDP Act). By using our platform, you consent to the practices described herein, designed to safeguard your data while enhancing your engagement with our skill-based activities.

1. Information Collection and Use

We collect minimal personal information to provide a seamless experience. This includes:

  1. Username and Email: To manage your account and participation in interactive challenges.
  2. Device Information: Such as device ID, operating system, and app usage data to ensure compatibility and improve performance.

We may collaborate with third-party services like Google Play Services, AdMob, and Firebase Analytics to enhance functionality, such as analytics and crash reporting. Your data is used solely to personalize your experience, manage challenges, and improve our platform.

Consent: By using YodhaPlay, you consent to the collection and use of your data as outlined in this policy. You may withdraw consent at any time by contacting us, though this may limit access to certain features.

2. Log Data

In the event of an app error, we collect diagnostic data to troubleshoot and improve our services. This log data may include:

  • Device IP address
  • Device name and operating system version
  • App configuration and usage timestamps
  • Other technical details relevant to the error

This information is used exclusively to resolve issues and maintain platform stability, and it is not shared with unauthorized parties.

3. Cookies

YodhaPlay does not directly use cookies. However, third-party services integrated into our platform (e.g., Firebase Analytics) may use cookies or similar technologies to enhance functionality. Cookies are small data files used as unique identifiers. You can accept or decline these cookies through your browser settings, though declining may affect some features of our platform.

4. Service Providers

We partner with third-party service providers to support our platform’s operations, including hosting, analytics, and error tracking. These providers may have access to your personal information to perform their tasks but are contractually obligated to:

  • Use your data only for the intended purpose.
  • Comply with the DPDP Act and other applicable privacy laws.
  • Not disclose or use your data for unauthorized purposes.

We carefully vet our service providers to ensure they meet our privacy and security standards.

5. Security

We prioritize your privacy and employ industry-standard security measures, such as encryption, to protect your personal information. However, no method of internet transmission or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to minimize risks, we cannot guarantee absolute security. We continuously update our protocols to enhance protection and comply with evolving legal standards.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, such as managing your account and complying with legal obligations. If you request deletion of your data, we will remove it from our systems within 30 days, unless retention is required by law. To request data deletion, please contact us at the details provided below.

7. User Rights

Under the DPDP Act, 2023, you have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  1. Right to Access: You can request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  2. Right to Correction: You can request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  3. Right to Deletion: You can request deletion of your data, subject to legal requirements.
  4. Right to Withdraw Consent: You can withdraw consent for data processing at any time.

To exercise these rights, please contact us at the details provided below.

8. Age and Regional Restrictions

YodhaPlay is intended for users aged 18 and above. We implement strict age verification processes to ensure compliance. Additionally, due to state-specific regulations, YodhaPlay is not available in Andhra Pradesh, Arunachal Pradesh, Assam, Odisha, and Telangana. We promote responsible engagement and encourage users to participate within their limits.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Any updates will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this policy regularly to stay informed about how we protect your data.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or your personal information, please contact us at:
