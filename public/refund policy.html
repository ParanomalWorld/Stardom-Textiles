Refund Policy

Introduction

Welcome to YodhaPlay, India’s leading skill-based esports platform for Free Fire tournaments. This Refund Policy outlines the conditions under which refunds are provided for tournament entry fees, in-app purchases, or other services on our platform. We are committed to transparency, fairness, and compliance with the Consumer Protection Act, 2019, and the Payment and Settlement Systems Act, 2007, ensuring a seamless experience for our warriors.

1. Eligibility for Refunds

Refunds may be requested under the following conditions, subject to review:

  1. Tournament Cancellations: If YodhaPlay cancels a tournament due to technical issues, insufficient participants, or other reasons, a full refund of the entry fee will be issued.
  2. Technical Issues: If you are unable to participate in a tournament due to platform-specific technical issues (e.g., server downtime), you may be eligible for a refund.
  3. In-App Purchases: Refunds for in-app purchases (e.g., premium features, virtual items) may be granted if the item is defective or not delivered as advertised.
  4. Unauthorized Transactions: Refunds will be issued for unauthorized or fraudulent transactions, provided you notify us within 7 days.

All refund requests are evaluated in accordance with Indian consumer protection laws.

2. Non-Refundable Scenarios

Refunds will not be provided in the following cases:

  • Completed Tournaments: Entry fees for tournaments you have participated in, regardless of your performance or rank.
  • Change of Mind: If you choose not to participate after registering, unless the cancellation is due to YodhaPlay’s fault.
  • Account Violations: No refunds will be issued if your account is suspended or terminated for violating our Terms & Conditions or Fair Play Policy.
  • Third-Party Purchases: Transactions processed through third-party platforms (e.g., Google Play, Apple App Store) are subject to their refund policies.

3. Refund Request Process

To request a refund, please follow these steps:

  1. Submit a Request: Contact our support team at support@yodhaplay.in or via our Help Center, including your account details, transaction ID, and reason for the refund.
  2. Timeframe: Requests must be submitted within 7 days of the transaction or issue.
  3. Review: Our team will review your request within 5 business days and notify you of the decision.
  4. Processing: Approved refunds will be processed within 7-10 business days to the original payment method, subject to payment provider policies.

4. Tournament Cancellations by YodhaPlay

In the event that YodhaPlay cancels a tournament, we will:

  • Issue a full refund of the entry fee to the original payment method.
  • Notify participants via email or in-app notifications.
  • Offer an option to transfer the entry fee to another tournament, if available.

Refunds for cancellations will be processed within 7 business days.

5. Technical Issues

If you encounter technical issues caused by YodhaPlay that prevent tournament participation, you may be eligible for a refund. To qualify:

  • Report the issue to support@yodhaplay.in within 24 hours.
  • Provide evidence (e.g., screenshots, error messages) to support your claim.

Our team will investigate and, if verified, issue a refund or credit for a future tournament within 7 business days.

6. Payment Processing

Refunds will be credited to the original payment method used for the transaction. Note:

  • Processing times may vary depending on your bank or payment provider (e.g., PhonePe, UPI).
  • Transaction fees (e.g., payment gateway charges) are non-refundable unless the issue is due to YodhaPlay’s error.

7. User Responsibilities

Users are responsible for:

  • Providing accurate payment information during transactions.
  • Complying with YodhaPlay’s Terms & Conditions and Fair Play Policy.
  • Contacting support promptly for refund requests or issues.

Failure to comply may result in denial of refund requests.

8. Changes to This Refund Policy

We may update this Refund Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Updates will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this policy regularly to stay informed.

9. Contact Us

For questions, concerns, or refund requests, please contact us at:

  • Email: support@yodhaplay.in
  • Support: Available 24/7 via our Help Center
